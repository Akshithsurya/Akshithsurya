# About Me

I'm **Akshith Surya**, a Grade 10 student from **Palakkad, Kerala**, passionate about **astrophysics**, **embedded systems**, **hardware security**, and building projects that solve real-world problems.

- NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025 Global Nominee
- ISRO Space Week 2025 – 1st Prize Winner
- Building AI-powered astronomy tools, embedded systems, cybersecurity projects, and developer platforms
- Interested in transit photometry, near-Earth objects, embedded Linux, firmware analysis, and aerospace research
- Hardware security researcher exploring ESP32, RF communication, and embedded firmware
- Working toward a future in astrophysics and aerospace engineering
